Groups that got effected by Nazis


Jews were affected heavily by the Nazis being in power. Hitler believed that Jews were a burden to society, and that true Germans (Aaryans) were way better than Jews. Jews had laws put in place so then life was harder for them, and then eventually the Holocaust happened, killing about 6 million Jews.

2 million Germans died from World War II, so millions of families had to go through the death of loved ones. Also men 16 - 60 were conscripted into the war, and some people didn't want that to happen so that had a huge effect on their mental health.

Women got affected because they didn't have any freedom. They were forced to have children so then Germany could have more boys so they could participate in the war.
